EternalMan

The title 'Eternal Man' reflects a fundamental concept in LDS theology, drawn from the teachings in the Book of Moses, concerning our eternal nature and addressing deep existential questions. This site also pays homage to Truman G. Madsen and his influential book "Eternal Man," which has inspired many to explore the profound depths of LDS theology and philosophy.

Purpose

Eternal Man" is dedicated to faithfuly exploring and sharing the profound theological and philosophical questions that arise from the restored gospel of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS). This platform delves into the rich tapestry of LDS doctrine, examining the eternal nature and potential of mankind, the purpose of mortal life, and the interplay between faith and reason
