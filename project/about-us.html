Purpose

Eternal Man" is dedicated to faithfuly exploring and sharing the profound theological and philosophical questions that arise from the restored gospel of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS). This platform delves into the rich tapestry of LDS doctrine, examining the eternal nature and potential of mankind, the purpose of mortal life, and the interplay between faith and reason

About Me

Welcome to "Eternal Man." I'm Cristobal Henriquez, a layman with a deep passion for exploring the profound depths of our faith. Though I am not formally trained in theology or philosophy, I am driven by a sincere curiosity and a desire to understand the complexities (or simplicity, depending on how you look at it) of Latter-day Saint teachings.

I'm just a regular guy who finds joy and awe in delving into the "meat" of our faith. While I firmly believe that Christ is the source of my testimony, my studies and reflections have undoubtedly strengthened my faith and deepened my appreciation for the vast scope of Heavenly Father's work. Each exploration brings me closer to understanding the divine plan and fills me with wonder at the intricacies of our eternal journey.

This site is a space where I share my journey and insights, not as an authority, but as a fellow seeker. I invite you to join me in exploring these rich and transformative ideas, and together, we can marvel at the divine wisdom that guides us all.
